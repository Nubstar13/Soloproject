Top 3 things to do in Seattle

Explore Pike Place Market

A must-visit for locals and tourists alike, Pike Place Market offers fresh seafood, unique crafts, and plenty of iconic photo ops (like the famous flying fish or the gum wall). Grab a coffee at the original Starbucks or enjoy a fresh bowl of clam chowder overlooking Elliott Bay.

Visit the Space Needle

Visit the Space Needle and Chihuly Garden and Glass Head to Seattle Center to check out the Space Needle’s incredible views of the city, Puget Sound, and Mt. Rainier. Right next door, Chihuly Garden and Glass showcases stunning glass sculptures that feel otherworldly.

Visit Discovery Park

Seattle’s largest green space offers over 500 acres of natural beauty. Walk the scenic trails, enjoy breathtaking views of the water and mountains, or relax on the quiet beach. It’s a perfect spot to escape the city without leaving it.
